About Us

What are our goals?

Our Mission

Creating a world where everyone can have access to food is our primary goal. As of 2019, the United Nations came out with 17 goals. Slice contributes to trying to accomplish the Goal 2 – “Zero Hunger”. With technology being, our future, we hope to combat the general issue of hunger, using Slice as a modern solution. Solving a massive problem like hunger requires us to look at the contributing factors that relate to the problem. Through research, we have discovered that, although massive amounts of food distributors and sources exist, almost 26% of the world is malnourished. Slice is a solution that triest reduce food waste by being able to distribute it to those who need it the most. Even with many charities and food banks available in and around Halifax, there are still several people that do not have access to food. Being students, we often fi ourselves on a budget and without enough money to eat out. We wanted to find a way to help people like us, while also contributed to society. We truly believe that Slice is one of the solutions that not only help its society but also with its users

Our Vision

Slice is an online forum that allows its users to post about excess food that they may have, which can then be used by someone else. The concept of “Social Sharing” in the aspect of being able to ultimately feed the community is what we want to achieve. People who throw conventions, workshops and events have the burden of being able to feed a massive crowd. Often, this results in excess amounts of food that tend to go to waste. Restaurants often have the second-largest community that is involved in food wastage. On one hand, you have so many people who lack the resources to obtain food and on the other hand, you have so many places that throw food out almost every day. Even If a part of this wasted food could go towards feeding the needy, it not only pushes our economy up but also brings us closer in achieving the the the development goal set by the United Nations

Who we are

As a group of students, we always live on a budget. This idea came about when we started attending seminars and events in our university where we realized that a lot of excess food used for these events was being thrown out. We wondered if many such events were happening in and around us where food like pizza and burgers were being thrown out, uneaten. Studying in a computer science field surrounded by technology all the time, we wondered if there was a way to create a solution for this problem which pushed us to brainstorm this idea. As a part of course work, we started building a prototype for the idea. As we created the pro-type we quickly realized that this project needed more than just the idea. We wanted to be able to get bigger restaurants involved, we wanted to test out the market place and we were made aware of the food policies that we needed to follow. So, understandably we did exactly those things. We first tested out the market with a set of surveys we added ou to our target customer range. We also spoke to the managers of many restaurants in and around Halifax about food wastage and their interest in the application. Based on both the responses we got we were able to create the prototype, which is Slice.
